## Official Description
Hackathon event held by Kementerian Keuangan Republik Indonesia to increase financial literacy among young generation in Indonesia through technology and innovation. 

## Breakdown
We got grouped randomly consist of one engineer, one designer, and one business person and within 24 hours we need to build a prototype of an application that can help people to understand about financial literacy and also we need to present it in front of the judges, including at the time the minister of finance of Indonesia, Sri Mulyani Indrawati.
