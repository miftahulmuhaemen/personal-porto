## Official Description
The event is the 15th National Student Qur'an Recitation Competition (Musabaqah Tilawatil Qur'an Mahasiswa Nasional XV). It was held at Universitas Brawijaya and Universitas Negeri Malang from July 28 to August 4, 2017. 

## Breakdown

We build Alqur'an app with few addition that I honestly cannot remember using Microsoft Visual Studio and I think Xamarin.
