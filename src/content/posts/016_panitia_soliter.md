## Official Description

The participation was in the National Computer Science Seminar (SOLITER) 2017.
The theme of the seminar was "The Role of E-Government in Realizing Good Governance Towards Smart City".
This event was organized by the Undergraduate Computer Science Program, Faculty of Mathematics and Natural Sciences - Universitas Lambung Mangkurat.
The event took place in Banjarbaru on October 12, 2017.
This certificate was signed by the Dean of the Faculty of Mathematics and Natural Sciences ULM, Lori di Santoso, M.Si, and the Head of the SOLITER Committee, Irwan Budiman, S.T., M.Kom.

## Breakdown

I served as a member of the organizing committee, I contributed to the planning and execution of the event, helping ensure that the seminar ran smoothly for all attendees and speakers.
